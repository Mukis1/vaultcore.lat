Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Our Commitment: VaultCore is committed to customer satisfaction. We offer a comprehensive 30-day return policy on most products, along with warranty coverage and support for defective items.

1. Return Eligibility

1.1 30-Day Return Window

You may return most products within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund, provided the following conditions are met:

  • The product is in its original, unused condition
  • All original packaging, accessories, and documentation are included
  • The product has not been activated, initialized, or configured with private keys
  • The tamper-evident seals remain intact and unbroken
  • You have the original proof of purchase (order confirmation or receipt)

1.2 Non-Returnable Items

The following items CANNOT be returned or refunded:

  • Hardware wallets that have been initialized or used with private keys (for security reasons)
  • Products with broken tamper-evident seals
  • Opened recovery phrase cards or seed phrase materials
  • Digital products, software licenses, or downloadable content
  • Gift cards or promotional items
  • Products purchased during final sale or clearance events (unless defective)
  • Products damaged due to misuse, abuse, or unauthorized modifications

1.3 Security and Safety Notice

Important Security Information: Due to the nature of cryptocurrency hardware wallets and the critical importance of security, we cannot accept returns of devices that have been:

  • Initialized with private keys or recovery phrases
  • Used to store or access cryptocurrency
  • Opened or tampered with in any way that compromises security

This policy protects both you and future customers from potential security vulnerabilities.

2. How to Initiate a Return

2.1 Return Request Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Contact our customer support team within 30 days of receiving your order

Step 2: Provide your order number and reason for return

Step 3: Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number via email

Step 4: Securely package the product with all original materials

Step 5: Ship the return to the address provided in your RMA confirmation

2.3 Return Shipping

Shipping Responsibility:

  • Customer Returns: You are responsible for return shipping costs for standard returns (non-defective items)
  • Defective Products: We cover return shipping costs for defective or damaged products

Risk of Loss: You bear the risk of loss during return shipment. We recommend using insured, trackable shipping methods. We are not responsible for lost or damaged return packages.

3. Refund Processing

3.1 Inspection and Approval

Once we receive your return:

  1. We will inspect the product within 3-5 business days
  2. We will verify that all return conditions are met
  3. We will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund
  4. Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days

3.2 Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method:

Payment Method Refund Processing Time
Credit/Debit Card 5-10 business days
PayPal 3-5 business days
Bank Transfer 7-14 business days

3.3 Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued in the following situations:

  • Product shows signs of use or wear
  • Original packaging is damaged or missing
  • Accessories or components are missing
  • Product is returned after 30 days but within warranty period

3.4 Refund Amounts

  • Product Price: Full refund of the purchase price
  • Shipping Costs: Original shipping costs are non-refundable (unless we made an error)
  • Return Shipping: Not refunded unless product is defective or we sent wrong item

4. Product Replacements

4.1 Replacement Options

We accept replacements for:

  • Different product models or versions
  • Different colors or configurations
  • Defective products (warranty replacement)

4.2 Replacement Process

To request a product replacement:

  1. Contact customer support to request a replacement
  2. Return the original product following standard return procedures
  3. Place a new order for the desired replacement product
  4. Once we receive and approve the return, we will refund the original purchase

Note: For defective warranty replacements, we will ship the replacement before receiving the defective unit in some cases.

5. Defective or Damaged Products

5.1 Reporting Defects

If you receive a defective or damaged product:

  • Report the issue within 7 days of receiving the product
  • Provide photographic evidence of the defect or damage
  • Include your order number and product details
  • Do not dispose of damaged packaging until authorized

5.2 Resolution Options

For defective or damaged products, we offer:

  • Replacement: Free replacement with expedited shipping
  • Refund: Full refund including original shipping costs
  • Repair: Professional repair service (for certain defects)

5.3 Shipping Damage

If your product arrives damaged due to shipping:

  • Document the damage with photographs immediately
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Retain all packaging materials
  • We will file a claim with the shipping carrier and provide a replacement
