About VaultCore

Swiss precision engineering meets cutting-edge cybersecurity

Our Story

Founded by Rudolf Reinhard Müller Rahm in 2017 in the heart of Switzerland's technology hub, VaultCore emerged from a vision to create the most secure hardware wallet solutions available. Our journey began when a team of cybersecurity experts and hardware engineers recognized the critical need for institutional-grade security in personal digital asset management.

Based in Zug and Zurich, we utilize Switzerland's reputation for precision, security, and innovation. Our manufacturing facilities adhere to the strictest quality control standards, and every device we produce undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our uncompromising security requirements.

Today, VaultCore serves over 50,000 clients worldwide, from individual cryptocurrency enthusiasts to major financial institutions. Our commitment to open-source transparency, continuous innovation, and customer education has established us as a trusted leader in the hardware wallet industry.
