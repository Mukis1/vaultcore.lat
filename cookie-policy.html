2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They allow websites to remember your preferences, track your activity, and improve your user experience. Cookies can be:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a specified period
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by the website you're visiting
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services and partners
