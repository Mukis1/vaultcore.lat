Enterprise-Grade Hardware Wallet Solutions

VaultCore represents the pinnacle of crypto hardware wallet technology, combining Swiss precision engineering with cutting-edge cybersecurity protocols. Our products are designed and manufactured in Switzerland's premier technology hub of Zug and Zurich, where innovation meets uncompromising quality standards. Each device undergoes rigorous testing and validation to ensure it meets the highest security requirements demanded by institutional investors and individual users alike.

Our hardware wallets utilize secure element chips certified to EAL6+ standards, the same level of protection used in passports and banking cards. The devices feature tamper-evident casing, secure boot processes, and isolated execution environments that create multiple layers of defense against physical and digital attacks. Every unit is manufactured in climate-controlled facilities with strict access controls, ensuring supply chain integrity from component sourcing to final assembly.

VaultCore devices support thousands of cryptocurrencies and tokens, featuring intuitive interfaces that make security accessible without compromising on protection. Our commitment to transparency is reflected in our open-source firmware, independently audited by leading security researchers. With regular security updates, comprehensive documentation, and dedicated support, VaultCore provides not just a product, but a complete security ecosystem. Backed by comprehensive warranties and Swiss quality guarantees, our wallets represent the ultimate solution for safeguarding digital assets in an increasingly connected world.
